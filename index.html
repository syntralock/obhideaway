Property Details

Refined Coastal Living

Nestled along the serene waterways of one of Orange Beach's most beautiful neighborhoods, 3835 Hideaway Street presents a rare opportunity to own a premier Gulf Coast retreat. Built in 2006 and thoughtfully maintained, this home harmonizes sophisticated design with the relaxed warmth of coastal living on a generous 6,250 sq ft lot with public canal access at the end of the street.

The open-concept main level draws you in with beautiful tile and engineered hardwood floors that flow seamlessly throughout, and walls of glass that frame sweeping views of the canal. A gourmet kitchen anchors the living spaces — outfitted with custom cabinetry and premium stainless appliances designed for those who love to entertain.

Step outside and you're just steps from the water. This home sits second to last on the street, with a public boat ramp at the end of the road leading directly into the canal — just 40 feet from public canal access puts boating, fishing, and paddling right at your doorstep.

Three spacious bedrooms — including a primary suite with spa-inspired bath — two full baths, and a convenient half bath ensure comfortable accommodations for family and guests alike. Generous under-house parking and a private driveway add everyday practicality. With the beach of the Gulf of America just a short drive away and The Wharf's dining and entertainment nearby, the best of Orange Beach is at your fingertips.
